Cloud

Moving our services to the cloud has a numerous advantages. With things like Office 365 it allows us to access our files from almost anywhere and have everything backed up at all times, it also makes sharing documents with other members of staff quicker. We are also using SharePoint to host our main internal websites and documentation, this makes everything easily accessible and updateable.

We are also moving to hosting some of our core services to the Microsoft Azure cloud which allows us to have all of the datacentre aspects for those applications handled off-site. This makes them cheaper to run and also allows them to be upgraded and expanded quicker and easier as Azure would handle most of the work involved in that. They can also be accessed from off-site easily as well with due to it being cloud hosted.

I am using SharePoint and office 365 regularly and am using and supporting the cloud hosted applications and am assisting in migrating others to the cloud.
